Richard designs interfaces: the metaphors that humanize them, and the systems that scale them. His work ranges from creating visual identity for Hoops Tournament to prototyping software solutions, like ShootShotBot. He studied Computer Science at the University of Ibadan. He started as a freelance Visual Designer before working at TQ and Geng. Most recently he contributed to Curious Pages, an app to build kickass websites from your phone. Email / Twitter / Instagram

sport branding

Hoops Tournament

Coming into the year, I created a logo and a few digital posters accompanying the launch of the tournament (a dynamic basketball league for Nigerian Universities) to promote audience engagement and brand awareness. Taking on the challenge of representing campus players as NBA stars, I made creative artworks with the players faces on each with a goal to make everyone on campus want to watch every match.

Flyer design

Du'Meta Dance Film

Created for a dance event, this flyer was carefully crafted to showcase elegance in the work of Taiwo Adeleye, CMA where he shares his experience and analysis using Laban Movement Analysis, his suggestions, and used his technique to create a short film.

Meaning and Inspiration

Du'Meta in English means 3 black men, a Film about Taiwo Adeleye's experience viewing a portrait titled Fred Ashton and Dancers displayed at New York's Gunggenheim Museum.

Instagram Carousel

A Quick Guide to Colors in Design

As a beginner, This post will show you all you need to know about the usage of colors in visual design for effective communication to your audience or customers.

Inspiration

Illustrations used in this post were inspired by the DreamWorks' popular movie, “Home”. The accurate use of color transitions to communicate feelings in the movie was the muse, so I decided to use cut-out versions of the characters to explain the topic with visual continuity in mind.

Features

Its performance went up on Instagram through features by influencer accounts.

View Post

For the fact that many tried to pick up skills during the lockdown, in which graphic design was among the top 3 on everyone's list, this got me investing into creating a couple more carousel posts really targeted at boosting the confidence of these early designers.

Other posts created are "Check Out This Kettle Design" and "3 Terms Most Designers Get Wrong".
